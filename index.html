For chlorine dispenser installation, verify the following:

✅ Dispenser is properly mounted at the borehole outlet
✅ Chlorine solution concentration is correct (0.2–0.5 mg/L residual)
✅ Flow rate is calibrated (1 push = 1 dose per 20L bucket)
✅ Community caretaker trained on usage and maintenance
✅ Initial stock of NaDCC tablets provided (min 3 months supply)
✅ GPS-tagged installation photo captured
✅ Dispenser serial number recorded

Take both a front and side photo of the dispenser for documentation.
